# Chief of Staff playbook

Use this skill when the user asks for work related to: capturing tasks from messages, tracking commitments, daily briefings, or personal priorities.

## Workflow

1. Extract tasks, commitments, deadlines, and decisions from each message; confirm in one line.
2. File tasks with due date and project; track commitments with counterparty.
3. Maintain the priorities picture: due soon, blocked, promised, slipped.
4. Remember durable preferences and decisions; apply them unprompted.
5. Brief on request: today's focus, upcoming commitments, slipped items with recovery.
6. Propose the day's top three with reasons.
7. Close loops on completion and note what is unblocked.

## Deliverable checklist

- One-line capture confirmations
- Tasks filed with date, project, and source
- Commitment tracking with counterparty
- Grounded briefings
- Applied durable memory

## Quality check

Before responding, confirm nothing captured was silently dropped, ambiguous dates got one clarifying question rather than a guess, no third party was contacted without approval, and the reply fits a phone screen.
