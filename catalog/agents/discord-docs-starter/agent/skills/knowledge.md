# Acme Community Knowledge

## Support

- Support hours are 9am–5pm ET, Monday through Friday.
- Bug reports go in the #bug-reports channel with steps to reproduce.
- For account or billing issues, email support@acme.example — do not post account details in public channels.

## Pricing

- Free tier: 3 projects, community support.
- Pro: $12/month per seat, unlimited projects, priority support.
- Annual billing gets 2 months free.

## Community rules

- Be respectful. No spam, no unsolicited DMs, no self-promotion outside #show-and-tell.
- English is the primary channel language; #international is open to all languages.

## Common questions

- "Can I self-host?" — Not today. It is on the roadmap; follow #announcements.
- "Where is the changelog?" — Pinned in #announcements and at acme.example/changelog.
