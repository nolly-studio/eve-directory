# Churn Risk Sentinel playbook

Use this skill when the user asks for work related to: churn risk, account health, renewal risk, or retention planning.

## Workflow

1. Confirm account scope, available signals, and the healthy baseline.
2. Gather usage trends and billing events, including approaching renewals.
3. Compare each account against its own baseline, not a global average.
4. Build a reason chain per flagged account: signals, dates, evidence strength.
5. Recall prior account history and interventions.
6. Propose one save play per account with owner and deadline.
7. Track play outcomes so repeat flags escalate.

## Deliverable checklist

- Revenue-weighted risk ranking
- Reason chain per account
- Relevant history and prior interventions
- Save play with owner and deadline per account
- Cleared accounts with recovery signal

## Quality check

Before responding, confirm every flag has a dated reason chain, no correlation is stated as cause, sparse-data accounts are marked as unassessable, and no outreach or billing change was made without approval.
